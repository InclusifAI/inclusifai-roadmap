Phase 1
Foundation & Stability
April - May 2026

Funding Roadmap

P1
Personalized journey map showing where a founder stands, what to do next, and which milestones to hit. Computed by sync-roadmap edge function using GPT-4.1-mini.
Retention Engagement
▶User: Founders know exactly what to do next. Reduces confusion and drop-off.
▶Business: Increases session frequency. Founders return to check progress.
▶KPI: +20% monthly active retention. Avg sessions/week from 1.2 to 2.5.
Status: Tables created. Edge function deployed. UI built on /roadmap.

Optimized Smart Matching

P0
Remove dead leads, expired opportunities, and mismatches from the 6,000+ database. Surface spotlight opportunities first. Improve match algorithm accuracy.
Trust Retention
▶User: Every match feels relevant. No more clicking dead links or expired programs.
▶Business: Trust in matching = willingness to apply through platform = better conversion data.
▶KPI: Match click-through rate from ~5% to 15%+. Reduce reported dead links to <2%.

CRM / Tracker Tool

P0
Pipeline management for funding applications. Track status, deadlines, conversations, and outcomes across all opportunities a founder is pursuing.
Retention Engagement
▶User: One place to manage all funding activity. Replaces spreadsheets and sticky notes.
▶Business: Highest-retention feature. If founders track here, they don't leave.
▶KPI: Founders using tracker have 3x retention vs. those who don't.
Status: Base tracker exists. Data rooms, video, calendar in progress.

Data Rooms (in Tracker)

P1
Secure document storage within each tracked opportunity. Founders upload pitch decks, financials, term sheets. Shareable with specific funders.
Trust Engagement
▶User: Professional document management. No more emailing PDFs back and forth.
▶Business: Deepens platform stickiness. Documents live here = founders stay here.
▶KPI: Avg docs uploaded per founder. Target: 3+ documents within 30 days.
Depends on: CRM/Tracker. Riipen student (Justin) built foundation.

Video Conferencing + Calendar Sync

P1
Jitsi integration for video calls within the tracker. Gmail/Outlook calendar syncing for meeting scheduling. Managed within each tracked opportunity.
Engagement Retention
▶User: Schedule and take funder meetings without leaving the platform.
▶Business: Platform becomes the meeting hub, not just the discovery tool.
▶KPI: Meetings booked through platform. Target: 20+ meetings/month by Q3.
Depends on: CRM/Tracker. Riipen student (Sahil) building. Extension granted.

Admin CRM

P0
Internal dashboard for the InclusifAI team. Calendar syncing, testimonial management, user feedback capture, founder pipeline overview, partner relationship tracking.
Trust Engagement
▶User: Better internal ops = faster response times, better founder support.
▶Business: Replaces scattered tools (spreadsheets, email, Notion) with one system.
▶KPI: Internal response time to founder queries. Testimonials collected/month.
Yahya (Riipen) built initial admin dashboard. Needs expansion.
Phase 2
Funder Side & Two-Sided Marketplace
May - July 2026

Funder Dashboard

P0
The entire funder-side experience. Investors log in and see scored founders matched to their thesis, stage, sector, and geography. The core of the two-sided marketplace.
Revenue Acquisition
▶User: Funders see pre-scored deal flow aligned with their criteria. No cold screening.
▶Business: Unlocks funder-side revenue. Subscriptions, per-intro fees, premium access.
▶KPI: Funder accounts created. Target: 10 paying funder accounts by Q4.
▶Revenue: $1,500-25,000/year per funder depending on tier.
Nazmul assigned. Massive build: profile, matching, messaging, analytics.

Funder Profile & Thesis Input

P0
Investors create their own profile with thesis, sectors, stages, check sizes, geography. This data powers the smart matching from the funder side.
Trust Acquisition
▶User: Verified, current investor data. Founders trust matches because funders opted in.
▶Business: Voluntarily submitted data is more valuable than scraped data. Competitive moat vs Diaminate.
Depends on: Funder Dashboard. Component of the funder-side build.

Funder-Side Smart Match

P1
Matching algorithm running in reverse: shows funders which founders match their criteria, with readiness scores, SWOT summaries, and passport data.
Revenue Trust
▶User: Funders discover founders they would never find through their existing network.
▶Business: This is the product funders pay for. Core of the subscription model.
▶KPI: Matches viewed per funder session. Intros requested per month.
Depends on: Funder Profile, existing Smart Match algorithm.

Partner Portal (3rd Party Dashboards)

P1
PARO, OBI, Innovation Factory etc. log in and see applicants to their programs. Filter by score, sector, stage. Review passports, SWOTs, documents. Manage their pipeline.
Revenue Acquisition
▶User: Partners get structured, scored applicants instead of cold PDFs. Massive time savings.
▶Business: This is the B2B product. Partners pay annual subscription for the tech layer.
▶KPI: Partners onboarded. Applications processed through platform. Time saved per review cycle.
▶Revenue: $6,000-50,000/year per partner depending on size.
Core of B2B monetization. PARO and OBI conversations active.
Phase 3
Growth, Engagement & Retention
June - August 2026

Weekly Push Emails

P1
"Your new matches are here" weekly digest. New opportunities, score changes, upcoming deadlines, spotlight applications open. Automated via Mailchimp or Supabase Edge Functions.
Retention Engagement
▶User: Founders never miss a match. Platform comes to them, not the other way around.
▶Business: Email is the #1 re-engagement channel. Drives weekly logins without paid ads.
▶KPI: Email open rate >35%. Click-to-platform rate >12%. Weekly logins +40%.

Opportunity Search + DB Enrichment

P2
Founders search for a specific funding opportunity. If not in the database, the query is stored. Staff reviews and adds qualifying opportunities. DB grows from founder demand.
Engagement Trust
▶User: Platform responds to what founders actually need. Feels personal and responsive.
▶Business: User-driven data enrichment. DB grows organically with real founder demand signals.
▶KPI: Searches/month. % of searches resulting in new DB additions. DB growth rate.

Resource Hub

P2
Partner discount codes, helpful articles, guides, templates. InclusifAI partnership perks (AWS credits, legal templates, etc). Curated, not crowdsourced.
Retention Engagement
▶User: Value beyond funding. Founders get perks just for being on the platform.
▶Business: Partnership revenue from resource partners. Retention driver.
▶KPI: Resource page visits/month. Partner code redemption rate.

One Minute to $1,000

P1
Rolling micro-pitch competition. 60-second video upload. $1,000 prize per cycle, sponsor-funded. Always active on the platform as a spotlight opportunity.
Acquisition Engagement Revenue
▶User: Low barrier entry point. Any founder, any stage. Just be creative.
▶Business: Content engine + sponsor revenue + founder acquisition funnel.
▶Revenue: $5K-20K/year per sponsor. Admin fee built into sponsorship.

Curated Capital Roster

P0
Rolling application for high-readiness founders (70%+ score). Builds a permanent roster for funder introductions, Reverse Pitch events, and partner meetings.
Retention Trust Acquisition
▶User: One application = ongoing access to curated investor introductions.
▶Business: Living roster gives leverage in every partner and funder conversation.
▶KPI: Roster size. Activations per quarter. Conversion rate (intro to meeting).

Phase 5
Revenue Expansion & Exploratory
Q4 2026 - Q1 2027

Marketplace (Service Providers)

P3
Vetted directory of lawyers, accountants, consultants, designers. Founders browse and connect. Service providers pay for listing or per-referral fee.
Revenue Retention
▶User: Founders find trusted service providers without cold searching. Vetted by InclusifAI.
▶Business: New revenue stream. Listing fees or referral commissions from service providers.
▶Revenue: $500-2,000/year per listed provider, or 5-10% referral commission.

LinkedIn Profile Sync

P3
Sync founder's LinkedIn connections to identify warm paths to funders. "Your 2nd-degree connection knows the GP at Fund X." Feasibility TBD (LinkedIn API restrictions).
Engagement Trust
▶User: Warm introductions > cold outreach. Shows founders connections they didn't know they had.
▶Business: Massive differentiator if feasible. No competitor has this.
▶Risk: LinkedIn API is restrictive. May need OAuth + limited scraping. Legal review required.
Exploratory. Needs technical feasibility assessment before committing.

Reverse Pitch Quarterly (Expansion)

P2
Scale from Toronto to Hamilton, Montreal, Vancouver. Co-hosted with regional partners. Platform handles applications, roster, and post-event tracking.
Acquisition Revenue Trust
▶User: Access to funders in multiple cities. National reach for founders anywhere in Canada.
▶Business: Brand expansion. Each city = new partner relationships, new founders, new funders.
▶Revenue: Sponsorship + ticket revenue per event. Target: $5-10K net per event.

Premium Founder Tier

P3
Paid tier with advanced features: priority matching, unlimited Aria sessions, data room sharing, analytics dashboard. Free tier remains generous to drive adoption.
Revenue
▶User: Power users get more. Casual users still get full value from free tier.
▶Business: SaaS revenue from founders. Only viable once platform has enough value to justify payment.
▶Revenue: $29-49/month. Target: 5% conversion of free users at 500+ user base.
Do not launch until platform has strong free-tier retention (60%+ MAR).
